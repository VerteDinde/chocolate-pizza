Chocolate Pizza

Print

Posted on 15, Dec 2013 / Desserts

For the fig-swirl: melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and start to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice.

Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1 lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/3 granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
