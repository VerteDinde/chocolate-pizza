For the fig-swirl: melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and start to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is barley lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin thicken or 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.
